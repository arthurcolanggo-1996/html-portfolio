Arthur's Top Movies

Here is the list of my top three movies of all time.

Avengers: End Game

Five years after Thanos wiped out half of all life in the universe, the remaining Avengers and their allies must travel through time to collect the Infinity Stones and bring back their loved ones. Along the way, they must face alternate versions of themselves, new threats, and the consequences of their past actions. In the end, they succeed in restoring the universe, but at a great cost.

Real Steal

In the near future, robot boxing has replaced human boxing as the most popular sport. Charlie Kenton, a former boxer, is now a small-time robot promoter who is struggling to make ends meet. When he is forced to take custody of his estranged son Max, Charlie reluctantly teams up with him to build and train a championship robot. Together, they learn the true meaning of sportsmanship and family.

Hidden Figures

Hidden Figures is a book and film about the lives of Katherine Johnson, Dorothy Vaughan, and Mary Jackson, three African-American women who worked as mathematicians at NASA during the Space Race. The women faced discrimination and segregation, but they persevered and made significant contributions to the agency's success. Katherine Johnson calculated the trajectory for Alan Shepard's Freedom 7 mission, the first American spaceflight; Dorothy Vaughan became the head of the West Computing Group, the first African-American woman to lead a computing unit at NASA; and Mary Jackson became an engineer, breaking the color barrier at NASA. Hidden Figures is a story of determination, perseverance, and the power of the human spirit.
